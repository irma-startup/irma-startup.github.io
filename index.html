What we do

Helping distribution network operators to manage their network through digitalization of low voltage networks.

Low voltage network are often unkown

Preventing network operators to properly manage them.

In the meantime, 200 millions smart meters will be deployed in europe by 2020

Opening the first opportunity to unlighten LV networks.

Our innovation

Extracting network information from the collected smart meter data. This has been made possible by bringing machine learning and signal processing into electrical studies.

Our derivated services

The network knowledge unlocks lots pf opportunities for network operators. Irma* has developed a set of services to help them managing LV networks:
  • Real time network monitoring
  • Predictive studies such as PV or EV impact prediction
  • Phase balancing to relief the network and postpone investments
