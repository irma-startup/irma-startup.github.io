The digital era of electrical networks

Automated digitalization of distribution networks
Using only smart meters data. No additional sensor. No paper archives.

We develop innovative tools for the digitalization of electrical distribution networks. This digitalization is key for a reliable and efficient network. It facilitates the integration of renewable production and electric vehicles. It enables network optimization, reducing losses and relieving stress points, while avoiding the replacement of electrical equipment. We work to support the energy transition

Low Voltage Networks
At the core of energy transition

Low Voltage networks are heavily impacted by the current deployment of renewable productions and electric vehicles. Improving their operation and planning is therefore crucial, but challenging: their millions of kilometers of lines have never been instrumented, while the available information is limited and often outdated.

This is why improving the observability of LV networks is essential to support the evolution toward smarter grids.
